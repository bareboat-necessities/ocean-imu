\section*{TimeVarGain NLO, VVR-only, no GNSS, no magnetometer}

This report shows the no-GNSS/no-magnetometer TimeVarGain nonlinear observer simulation.
The observer uses the vertical VVR constraint and IMU measurements only. Yaw is free and is not interpreted as an observable output.
